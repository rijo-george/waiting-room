How It Works

Stupid simple.
By design.

1

Add an open loop

Tap + or press A. Type who and what. Optional: expected date and a note. That's it.

2

Watch it age

Green (fresh), yellow (getting old), red (overdue). The color tells you what needs attention before you even read it.

3

Nudge or resolve

Followed up? Log it. Done? Resolve it — swipe on iPhone or press R on Mac. It moves to history with a receipt.
