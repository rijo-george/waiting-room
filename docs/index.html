How It Works

Simple by design.

1

Add an item

Press A (or tap +). Enter who, what, and optionally when you expect it. Pick your direction — are you waiting, or are they?

2

Watch it age

Items turn green → yellow → red as time passes. At a glance, you know what's overdue. No calendar needed.

3

Nudge when needed

Press N to log a follow-up with a note. "Sent reminder email." "Called, left voicemail." Build the paper trail.

4

Resolve and get a receipt

Press R to mark it done. You get a receipt — who, what, how long, how many nudges. Copy it, share it, archive it.
